Kort

An OpenStreetMap Game

  • follow @KortGame

OpenStreetMap

Kort helps to improve OpenStreetMap-data. Missions to be solved are shown on a map. As soon as enough equal missions have been solved, the solution of a mission will be automatically inserted in OpenStreetMap.

Players can check already fulfilled tasks regarding their correctness.

Gamification

Kort uses Gamification to motivate users to play with the app. By solving missions, points (so called Koins) can be earned. The goal is to continually rise in ranking. Additionally, the player is awarded medals for his efforts.

App

Kort is a native app written in React Native and is therefore available on the major mobile plattforms Android and iOS.

Kort is currently being developed and will be available in Q2 2017. Stay updated on GitHub.
